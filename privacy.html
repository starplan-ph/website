PRIVACY POLICY

This Policy explains what information we collect when you use Starplan's mobile application. It also has information about how we store, use, transfer, and delete that information. Our aim is not just to comply with privacy law. It's to earn your trust.

Starplan doesn't make money from ads. So we don't collect data in order to advertise to you. The tracking we do at Starplan is to make our product work as well as possible.

In order to give you the best possible experience using Starplan, we collect information from your interactions with our network. Some of this information, you actively tell us (such as your email address, which we use to track you account or communicate with you). Other information, we collect based on actions you take while using Starplan such as what pages you access and your interactions with our product features (like current location, bus data, and passenger count data). This information includes records of those interactions, your Internet Protocol address, and information about your device.
